## Muc tieu

Lab 2 cai dat 5 thuat toan mat ma hoc co dien va trien khai thanh API bang Flask de test tren Postman.

## Cach em tu chinh bai de tranh giong mau co san

- Tu viet lai cau truc thu muc `lab2/cipher/`
- Them xu ly loi cho JSON va tham so dau vao trong `app.py`
- Doi bo du lieu test sang noi dung gan voi thong tin hoc tap cua em
- Them `self_check.py` de tu kiem tra tung thuat toan truoc khi demo
- Them file `postman_collection.json` de import truc tiep vao Postman

## Cach tu demo bai

1. Chay `python lab2/self_check.py`
2. Chay `python lab2/app.py`
3. Import `lab2/postman_collection.json` vao Postman
4. Gui lan luot cac request va chup man hinh ket qua
